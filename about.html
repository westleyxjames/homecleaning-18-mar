About BrightPath Cleaning Co

Our Story

BrightPath Cleaning Co was established to provide residential cleaning services in the New York area. We started with a simple approach: focus on thorough cleaning and respect for our clients' homes.

Over time, we have served numerous residences across New York, learning and adapting our services based on customer feedback and cleaning needs.

Today, we continue to provide cleaning services for homes throughout the area, working to maintain the same standards of service that we started with.
