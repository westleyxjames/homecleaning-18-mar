Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our residential cleaning services

BrightPath Cleaning Co provides residential cleaning services throughout New York City and surrounding areas. We serve clients in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location, as travel time and accessibility may affect scheduling in some areas.

We offer several types of residential cleaning services including standard home cleaning, deep cleaning services, move-in/move-out cleaning, apartment cleaning, and recurring cleaning plans. Each service is designed to meet different needs and can be customized based on your specific requirements.

Cleaning time varies based on home size, service type, and current condition. A standard cleaning for an average-sized home typically takes 2-4 hours. Deep cleaning and move-in/move-out services require more time. We provide time estimates during the initial consultation based on your home's specifics.

We bring standard cleaning supplies and equipment to each appointment. If you have specific products you'd like us to use or areas that require special cleaning solutions, please inform us during scheduling. We can accommodate preferences for certain cleaning products when possible.

You can choose whether to be present during the cleaning. Many clients provide access instructions and are away during service, while others prefer to be home. We work according to your preference and can discuss access arrangements during scheduling.

You can schedule a cleaning service by contacting us through our contact form, calling us at +1-398-392-3398, or emailing cleaing@brightpathcleaningco.site. We will discuss your cleaning needs, home specifics, and preferred schedule to arrange an appropriate service time.

Standard cleaning includes dusting of accessible surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors, bathroom cleaning and sanitizing, kitchen surface cleaning, trash removal, and general tidying of spaces. This service focuses on regular maintenance of your home's cleanliness.

Deep cleaning includes all standard cleaning tasks plus additional detailed work. This includes baseboard and trim cleaning, light fixture cleaning, cabinet exterior cleaning, appliance exterior detailing, window sill and track cleaning, behind and under movable furniture, and detailed bathroom grout attention. Deep cleaning is more thorough and takes more time.

We understand that schedules can change. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule an appointment. We will work with you to find an alternative date and time that works for both parties. Our cancellation and rescheduling policy will be explained when you book service.

The frequency of recurring cleaning depends on your household needs, lifestyle, and preferences. Common schedules include weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleaning. We can help you determine an appropriate schedule during consultation based on factors like home size, number of occupants, and your cleanliness standards.

Payment details and accepted methods will be discussed when you book your service. We aim to provide convenient payment options for our clients. For specific questions about payment, please contact us directly.
