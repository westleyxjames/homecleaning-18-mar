Our Service Offerings

Standard Home Cleaning

Our standard home cleaning service provides regular maintenance for your living spaces. This service is designed for homeowners who want consistent upkeep of their home's cleanliness. Our team follows a systematic approach to ensure that all essential areas receive proper attention during each visit.

What's Included:

  • Dusting of accessible surfaces
  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs
  • Mopping hard floors
  • Bathroom cleaning and sanitizing
  • Kitchen surface cleaning

Deep Cleaning Services

Deep cleaning goes beyond regular maintenance to address areas that may require extra attention. This service is suitable for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while, or for seasonal thorough cleanings.

What's Included:

  • All standard cleaning tasks
  • Baseboard and trim cleaning
  • Light fixture cleaning
  • Cabinet exterior cleaning
  • Window sill and track cleaning

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Moving is a significant undertaking, and cleaning during this transition can be overwhelming. Our move-in/move-out cleaning service is designed to prepare a property for new occupants or help you leave your previous residence in good condition.

What's Included:

  • Comprehensive cleaning of all rooms
  • Cabinet and closet interior cleaning
  • All appliance cleaning
  • Detailed bathroom and kitchen work
